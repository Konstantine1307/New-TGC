The Cottage

The Cottage offers warm and cosy self catering accommodation with everything a family or friends would need for their holiday.

This 2 bedrooms holiday cottage offers ample kitchen facilities, great shower, tranquil private and shared gardens in an area close to the beach.

Kitchen, utility room, large open plan dining/sitting room with feature cast iron wood burning stove and sofa-bed, large walk-in shower room and separate toilet. In the first floor 2 bedrooms with views over the garden towards Wheal Grey Lake.
