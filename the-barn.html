The Barn

The Barn offers bright and characterful self catering accommodation with traditional features. Decorated and appointed to provide a warm stay with up to date appliances.

Fully equipped kitchen including washer dryer, open plan living / dining room with sofa and open staircase.

Double bedroom with kingsize bed or twin beds plus guest pull out bed. Views over Wheal Grey Lake. Modern bathroom with walk-in shower.
