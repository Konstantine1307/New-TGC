The Barn

The Barn offers bright and characterful self catering accommodation with traditional features, ideal for a couple, possibly with a child. The Barn has been decorated and appointed to reflect our wish to provide a warm stay with up to date appliances.

With fully equipped kitchen including washer dryer, open plan living / dining room with sofa and open staircase.

in the first floor, double bedroom with kingsize bed or twin beds plus guest bed recommended for a child with view over Wheal Grey Lake, newly refurbished bathroom with walk-in shower.

  • Max: 2 Adults + 1 Child under 8 years old
  • Size: 35 m2
  • View: Lake & Garden Views
  • Bed: 1 King Size bed or 2 Twin Beds + a pull out Bed
